Conference Overview

Diplomacy. Leadership.
Global Dialogue.

Uttam Model United Nations 3.0 is the third edition of Uttam School for Girls' flagship MUN conference in Ghaziabad. Designed to emulate the professionalism of international diplomatic summits, the conference empowers delegates to engage in policy discussions, negotiation, public speaking, and collaborative problem-solving.

With a legacy of impactful debate and remarkable delegate participation, Uttam MUN continues to establish itself as a distinguished platform for young diplomats across schools.
